structures.fyi was created by Taylor Stevens. The purpose of this site is to make learning about structural biology easy, enjoyable, and accessible to those with only a basic understanding of biology and chemistry. Learning structural biology through verbal descriptions and pictures can be confusing. A 3D physical representation of macromolecular structure that can be manipulated by the user can make the structures and their behavior much easier to understand.

If you are a structural biologist and would like to collaborate on a structural presentation of your work please contact me at:
