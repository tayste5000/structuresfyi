Structure of the month:
Constituitive Andorstane Recptor (CAR) (PDB ID: 1XNX)

CAR is a nuclear hormone receptor present in the liver. CAR is part of the liver's xenobiotic regulation mechanism. Xenobiotic regulation involves detecting foreign molecules and either destroying them or removing them from the cells. This mechanism is abused in drug resistant cancer to reduce the effectiveness of chemotheraputics.
